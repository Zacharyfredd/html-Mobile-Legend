Layla Story

Layla, a young and mischievous member of the Eruditio Rangers, is known for her bravery and the honorary title of "Shining Star" for protecting the Starlium Reactor during a vicious attack on Eruditio. She grew up in Eruditio, known for her helpfulness and sometimes mischievous nature, often assisting scholars and traders.
