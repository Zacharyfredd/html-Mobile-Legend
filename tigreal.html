Tigreal Story

Tigreal is the Captain of the Moniyan Empire's Imperial Order of Knights. Even as a child, he wanted nothing more than to become a great knight of the Empire. From his youth Tigreal was a loyal believer in the Lord of Light. He swore to become a member of the Knight's Order to defend the status and symbol of the Moniyan Empire throughout the Land of Dawn.

Tap again: Launches a second attack, dealing 280 / 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 (+60% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and knocking them up for 1 second.

    5. Forcefully thrust his sword into the earth, pulling surrounding enemies to himself. Then he deals 270 / 350 / 430 (+130% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and stunning them for 1.5 seconds. This skill can be interrupted by transformation and knock up effects.
